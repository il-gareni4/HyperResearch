[b]Hyper Research[/b] enhances Terraria's [i]Journey mode[/i] with quality-of-life features.
The mod gives you a lot of settings in the config, so take a look there and customize the mod for yourself.

[h1]Core Gameplay Enhancements[/h1]
[list]
[*][b]Effortless Research:[/b] Automatically research items directly from your inventory â€“ no need to constantly open the Research Menu.
[*][b]Smart Auto-Research:[/b] Automatically research craftable (required materials and have access to the necessary crafting stations), shimmerable and decraftable items.
[*][b]Inventory Cleaning:[/b] Automatically (or manualy) clean researched items from your inventory (configurable).
[*][b]Stay Informed:[/b] Receive notifications for all newly researched items grouped by the research source.
[*][b]Convenient Controls:[/b] Access features through helpful UI buttons and customizable keybinds.
[*][b]Multiplayer Ready:[/b]
    [list]
    [*]Share research progress with your team.
    [*]Configure server-wide settings for a consistent experience.
    [/list]
[/list]

[h2]Additional Quality of Life[/h2]
[list]
[*][b]Modifier Selector:[/b] Choose item modifiers directly within the duplication menu.
[*][b]Shop Auto-Research:[/b] Automatically research the contents of NPC shops.
[*][b]One-Tap Loot Research:[/b] Research the entire contents of bags, crates, and other loot containers instantly.
[*][b]Infinite Consumables:[/b] Infinite stacks of researched consumables like potions, blocks, throwing weapons, and more (configurable categories).
[*][b]World-Wide Banner Buffs:[/b] Gain the buff of researched monster banners without needing to place them.
[*][b]Unlimited Potion Buffs:[/b] Gain the buff of researched potions/food/flasks without consuming them.
[*][b]Custom Research Numbers:[/b] Adjust the number of items needed for research for any specific item in the game.
[/list]

[h3]Extra Conveniences[/h3]
[list]
[*][b]Universal Tooltip:[/b] See a custom research tooltip that works even with other mod item slots.
[*][b]Progress Tracker:[/b] Easily view how many items you've researched out of the total available.
[*][b]Stack Management:[/b] Automatically stack researched items to their maximum capacity.
[*]Optionally set the research requirement for duplication to just one item.
[/list]
